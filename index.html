  • Touch screen menu – if one does not like voice commands
  • Directions are spoken and shown on screen
  • Print entry tickets/labels
  • Arrange visits in advance – sent entry tickets by email
  • Video call function - you can speak to desired office secretary
  • Multilanguage support – English and local language(-s)

Best Virtual Office Assistant

Artificial intelligence based virtual office assistant system for business centers. It can increase productivity of your existing secretaries or fully replace them. It is an information kiosk like device with voice control, touch screen, camera, speakers and ticket printer. Customer can contact desired office via video call feature and representative can let them. People also can ask information about toilets, lifts on premises/nearby cafes and restaurants, parking, etc.

Voice recognition

The device has built in voice recognition and can be controlled via voice command, for example “where are toilets”, “can I contact company X”, “where I can have a cup of coffee” or event “tell me a joke”

Face recognition

Device actives as soon as camera detects a person facing/looking at the device and standing in front.

Video call function

Device can make full HD crisp quality video calls to office representative. Both parties will be able to see and hear each other.
