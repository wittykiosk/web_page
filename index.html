Best Virtual Office Assistant

Artificial intelligence based virtual office assistant system for business centers. It can increase productivity of your existing secretaries or fully replace them. It is an information kiosk-like device with voice control, touch screen, camera, speakers and ticket printer. Customer can contact desired office via full HD video call feature and representative can let them. People also can ask information about toilets, lifts on premises/nearby cafes and restaurants, parking, etc.

Product Features

State of the art technologies makes this project shine among similar products

Voice recognition

The device has built in voice recognition and can be controlled via voice command, for example “can I contact company X”.

Face recognition

Device actives as soon as camera detects a person facing/looking at the device and standing in front.

Video call function

Device can make full HD crisp quality video calls to office representative. Both parties will be able to see and hear each other.

Video call function

Device can make full HD crisp quality video calls to office representative. Both parties will be able to see and hear each other.

Advance visitor

If office is equipped with entry gates, visitor can be sent advance electronic ticket with unique QR code.

Ticket printer

Device can be equipped with ticket printer and visitor will be issued physical entry ticket for entry and exit.

Multi language

Device can be interacted by multiple languages: English and any other. 3 languages can be supported as well.

Cloud technologies

We use most advanced Google and Amazon cloud technologies in our solution to ensure reliability of our service

Touch screen

Use intuitive web like touch interface if you having problems communicating using voice commands.

Offline mode

If device goes offline it detect it and show direct telephone numbers of each company.
